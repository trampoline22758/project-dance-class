Dance Your Heart Out at Miss Kim's Dance Class!

At Miss Kim's Dance Class in Chesterfield, MO, we turn passion into performance. Our talented and certified instructors create a nurturing environment where dancers of all ages can thrive. Whether you're a beginner exploring the world of dance or an experienced performer refining your technique, you'll find a welcoming home here.

We offer fair pricing, small class sizes, and a conveniently located studio—making now the perfect time to start dancing. From ballet to hip-hop, and even competitive teams, we provide a wide range of dance styles for every skill level. Parents can relax in our comfortable viewing area or use our breakroom, complete with a microwave, fridge, and freezer. With flexible scheduling and over 30 years of experience, we’ve been the trusted choice for dancers since 1994.

Why Choose Miss Kim's Dance Class?

Choosing Miss Kim's Dance Class means joining a community that values creativity, technique, and self-expression. Our experienced instructors bring decades of teaching expertise, ensuring every student receives personalized attention in a positive, supportive environment.

We offer transparent pricing, flexible scheduling, and diverse styles to suit every dancer’s needs. With our no-drama code of conduct, you can trust that your experience will be positive and enriching. Call us today or join a free trial dance class to see why we're considered the best dance school in Chesterfield.
