    Dance Your Heart Out at Miss Kim's Dance Class!

    At Miss Kim's Dance Class in Chesterfield, MO, we turn passion into performance. Our talented and certified instructors create a nurturing environment where dancers of all ages can thrive. Whether you're a beginner exploring the world of dance or an experienced performer refining your technique, you'll find a welcoming home here.

    We offer fair pricing, small class sizes, and a conveniently located studio—making now the perfect time to start dancing. From ballet to hip-hop and competitive teams, we provide a wide range of dance styles. Parents can relax in our comfortable viewing area or use our breakroom with a microwave, fridge, and freezer. Since 1994, families have trusted us for flexible scheduling and expert instruction.

    Why Choose Miss Kim's Dance Class?

    Join a community that values creativity, technique, and self-expression. Our experienced instructors bring decades of teaching expertise, ensuring every student receives personalized attention in a positive, supportive environment.

    We offer transparent pricing, flexible scheduling, and diverse styles to suit every dancer. With our no-drama code of conduct, your experience will be positive and enriching. Book a free trial dance class and see why we’re Chesterfield’s favorite dance school.
